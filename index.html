Tailblazing deeper and scalable cellular biology.

Hi, my name is Duo Peng. I'm a senior computational biologist at the Chan Zuckerberg Biohub San Francisco, where I lead a team building bioinformatics solutions that:

  • Map the human subcellular proteome architecture through organellar mass spectrometry.
  • Automate CRISPR informatics pipelines to accelerate cell biology discovery at scale.
  • Mine, integrate, and model omics datasets at the repository scale, encompassing entire species and large-scale perturbation datasets.
